                          Architecture

                          Privacy-first by design

                          1. Multi-layer detection

                          AI Shield combines a local Naive Bayes classifier, a rule engine, and behavior modeling to detect phishing, scams, impersonation, and coercion patterns.

                          2. Minimal encrypted logging

                          No storage happens without consent. Logged records are encrypted, anonymized, and limited to the smallest data footprint needed for auditability.

                          3. Explainable decisions

                          Every verdict includes reasons, highlighted patterns, and recommended next steps so users understand why a message was flagged.

                          4. Ready for extension

                          The API is modular and prepared for future voice-scam detection, browser extensions, additional models, and mobile integration.
